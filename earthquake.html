Earthquake Safety Guide

Before an Earthquake (Precautions)

  • Build earthquake-resistant structures.
  • Secure heavy objects and furniture.

During an Earthquake (Handling the Event)

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On under sturdy furniture.
  • Stay indoors; avoid windows and falling objects.

After an Earthquake (Recovery)

  • Check for injuries and provide first aid.
  • Look for gas leaks, fires, or structural damage.
